Gentoox Loader: Net Boot

This wizard will guide you through all you need to do to upload a kernel and initrd via your web-browser in order to boot your Xbox.  Please click "Next ->" to proceed.

Key things to note are that uploading files (especially large ones) may take a long time and that you must not at any time use the "Forward", "Back", "Stop" or "Refresh" buttons on your browser as this will most likely cause errors and require you to reboot your Xbox and start again.  Also note that filenames are totally irrelevant since all files are loaded directly into RAM - that is to say, you may call your kernel and initrd files anything you wish.
